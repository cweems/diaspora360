About Us



Bethel Alemu has over two years of experience supporting multiple offices within USAID with several Knowledge Management (KM) activities. Ms. Alemu is currently a Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E) specialist at the QED Group, LLC where she supports the USAID Knowledge Driven Microenterprise Development (KDMD) Project. Prior to her work at the QED Group, Ms. Alemu worked for the Center on Budget and Policy Priorities (CBPP) doing research for the International Budget Partnership initiative. Ms. Alemu has also previously worked in Sierra Leone where she served on the editorial team to publish the 2010 country Millennium Development Goal Report for Sierra Leone. Originally from Ethiopia, she has also spent considerable time living and studying in Zimbabwe. She has Bachelor of Science in Economics and a Minor in Business Administration from Northeastern University. Ms. Alemu is also currently finishing up her Master of Arts in International Development Studies and Economic Development from the George Washington University.
